gliese art collective

Here's my current project: Gliese Art Collective.

 
To inspire equity through art. The Collective is about showcasing artists who have been "othered". We educate publicly, inspire discourse, and analyze history and the present and the way they connect. In order to do this, we will create accessible videos, modules, and community events. Check out our mission statement, Instagram, and Tumblr. Meet our staff!
